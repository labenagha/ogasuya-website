Cooking on Site & Buffet

Our standard service includes 2 servers and 1 Aboki but this number varies depending on the number of guests and duration of the event. For larger events, we are happy to set up multiple buffet lines so your guests do not need to wait long before eating. The buffet line will be set up so our servers serve your guests. We utilize this style to avoid cross-contamination and allergen exposure.
This also gives us the opportunity to chat and educate your guests about their meal. Our staff typically require a minimum of 2 hours of set-up time and will stay about an hour after the line dies down and guests have all had a chance to come back for seconds. Our staff will clean all food waste but may not stay until the end of your event. All leftovers will be packaged into disposable containers, labeled with the name of the item and CONTANS PEANUT, and left for you to carry home.

Pick up or Delivery

We are happy to offer both pick-up and delivery options for our customers. These options are best suited for smaller orders between 20-50 people. You will be responsible for keeping the food warm after pick up. We are happy to provide reheating instructions, if needed. Order can be packaged individually per person or in disposable steam trays suited for full-sized rectangle chaffing dishes. If requested, we can provide cutlery, napkins, plates, toothpicks, and extra suya powder. We ask for 7 days notice but can sometimes accommodate more urgent needs.
